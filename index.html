Graphic Design & Art

I started drawing ever since I was young. In 2016, I met a friend who inspired me to dive deeper into art. Since then, I have bought art materials, graphite pencils, sketchbooks, coloring materials, and more. In 2019, I discovered digital art. It has been my primary medium, and I started with a mouse to draw. It wasn't easy until I earned enough money to buy a tablet. My passion for drawing has introduced me to different disciplines, not only limited to sketching but also layouts and designing.

Skills Acquired: Character Design, Logo Design, Web Design, Web Comic, Pixel Art, Traditional Art

Tools Used: Clip Studio Paint, Adobe CC (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, XD, After Effects), Figma, Canva

Poetry

One of my favorite pastimes is writing poetry. I don't remember the last time I wrote poems but writing poems wires my brain to think and be more imaginative in creatively using words. As an artist, I also wanted to be creative in writing. Whenever I feel surges of emotions, anger, love, happiness, fear, etc. I write poems to express myself creatively.
